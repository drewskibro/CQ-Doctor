FAQs

Common questions about Wegovy

Clinical trials show patients lose up to 20.7% of their body weight over 68 weeks. Most patients lose 2-3 stone within 6-12 months. Individual results vary based on starting weight, adherence to treatment, and lifestyle factors.

Wegovy comes in a pre-filled pen that's easy to use. You inject it once weekly under the skin of your stomach, thigh, or upper arm. The needle is very small and most patients report minimal discomfort. We provide full instructions and video guides with your first delivery.

The most common side effects are nausea, diarrhea, reduced appetite, vomiting, and constipation. These are usually mild and improve over time as your body adjusts. Starting with a low dose and gradually increasing helps minimize side effects. Our prescribers monitor you closely and can adjust your dose if needed.

Most patients take Wegovy for 6-12 months to reach their weight loss goals. Some continue longer for weight maintenance. Your prescriber will work with you to determine the right duration based on your progress and goals. You can stop anytime, though gradual tapering is recommended.

Wegovy is suitable for adults with a BMI of 30+ (or 27+ with weight-related health conditions). It's not suitable if you're pregnant, breastfeeding, or have certain medical conditions. Complete our online assessment and our prescribers will determine if Wegovy is right for you based on your medical history.
